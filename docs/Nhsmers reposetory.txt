[About NHSM]
[[School Description]]Established by presidential decree in 2021, the National Higher School of Mathematics (NHSM) is Algeria’s first institution dedicated to awarding engineering degrees in mathematics. Strategically located at the Sidi Abdellah technological hub in Algiers, the school serves as a center of excellence for the nation’s future scientific leaders. To maintain its high academic standards, NHSM exclusively recruits top-tier Baccalaureate graduates from the Mathematics, Experimental Sciences, and Technical Mathematics streams.
[Link to school website] https://nhsm.dz/
[Road map for the school]NHSM offers a 5-year program to all students. It starts with 2 preparatory years, followed by a common core year. Finally, students spend their last 2 years in their chosen specialties (Modeling and Simulation, Cryptography Coding and Security, Statistics Econometrics for Actuarial Sciences, Mathematical Physics). Students also have the chance to participate in the prestigious international program IMM (International Masters in Mathematics).[Link to IMM website] https://internationalmathematicsmaster.org/program-overview/algeria/
[[Student Life]]
[[[Clubs]]]NHSM hosts a number of clubs in different fields such as: Eureka Club, M4T Club, Stratos Club, 8squared Club, and Finmatics Club.
[[[Campus]]]Students reside at 2 residencies in the Student Hub (Mahelma 3 for boys and Mahelma 4 for girls, shared with ENSIA students) and have access to the full properties of the technological pole (Main Library, Student Hub).
The Residency: Hosting 1,000 students per residency, giving students clean individual rooms equipped with a bed, mattress, pillow, desk, and chair. The residency features showers, a large courtyard, an on-campus shop, a university restaurant (resto), a stadium, study rooms, a TV room, and a doctor's office.
The Pole:Student Hub: Contains the general restaurant, doctors' offices, a laundry room, a shop, a cafeteria, and a dedicated resting area.Main Library: Features quiet study rooms open to all pole students and large resting spaces.
